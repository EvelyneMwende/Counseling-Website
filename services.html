Career Guidance

It is where we assist you in making and implementing educational and occupational choices. An individual is shown how to develop your skills, self-knowledge, education and occupational exploration, career planning. The program is based on individual assessments of abilities, interests, career needs, and goals; communication of information concerning career options and opportunities with the organization; and career counseling to set realistic goals and plan for their attainment.


Behavoiral Modification

A therapeutic approach designed to change a particular negative behavior.We as a team use positive reinforcement to improve the behavior. We offer Three main types of therapy: Cognitive behavioral therapy that is centered on a person's thoughts and beliefs and how they influence their actions and moods. The long-term goal is to solve current problems until a person's thinking and behavioral patterns become healthier. Rational emotive behavior therapy (REBT) that focuses on identifying negative or destructive thoughts and feelings. People then actively challenge those thoughts and replace them with more rational, realistic ones. Cognitive therapy helps clients recognize negative thoughts and errors in logic that cause depression. The clients are guided to challenge the thoughts and then try new interpretations and apply alternatives and healthier ways of thinking.


Marriage Counseling

Marriage counseling, also called couples therapy, is a type of psychotherapy. Through marriage counseling, you can make thoughtful decisions about rebuilding and strengthening your relationship or going your separate ways. Marriage counseling helps couples of all types recognize and resolve conflicts and improve their relationships. Marriage counseling might also be helpful in cases of domestic abuse. Marriage counseling typically includes both partners, but sometimes one partner chooses to work with a therapist alone. The specific treatment plan depends on the situation. You can use marriage counseling to help with many specific issues, including: Communication problems, Sexual difficulties, Conflicts about child rearing or blended families, Substance abuse, Anger, Infidelity.


Financial Management Advice

We help you get better managerial skills in the area of financial decision making. This applies to both long term and short-term goals of how you spend your income. It also deals with giving advice on how to improve on saving and the procurement of income to support your lifestyle while still effectively utilizing what you have. Our counsellors use different ways to first analyze how you use your money like using Kakeibo (Japanese art of saving money).
